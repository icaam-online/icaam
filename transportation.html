Transportation

Traveling to the Hotel in Kiris, Türkiye

There are several ways to travel to the hotel located in Kiris, Türkiye:

  1. Arrival in Antalya:

You can reach Antalya, Türkiye through the following options:

  • By Air:
    • Direct Flights: Antalya Airport (AYT) is a major international airport with direct flights from many cities around the world.
    • Connecting Flights: If no direct flights are available, you can fly to Istanbul Airport (IST) and then take a domestic flight to Antalya.
  • By Land:
    • Bus: Antalya is well connected by bus to major cities in Türkiye and neighboring countries like Greece or Bulgaria.
  1. Transportation from Antalya Airport to Kiris:

Once you arrive at Antalya Airport (AYT), you have these transportation options to reach the hotel in Kirs:

  • Hotel's Round-trip Airport Transfer: The hotel offers convenient round-trip transportation for €130 total for 1-2 people and €40 per person for groups of 3 or more.
  • Taxi: Taxis are readily available at the airport. The journey takes about 1 hour 20 minutes (67 km).
  • Car Rental: You can consider renting a car at the airport.
