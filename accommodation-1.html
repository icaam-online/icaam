Full Board includes coffee breaks, breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The hotel fee includes the welcome gala.

Prepayment for the hotel

Those who wish to make a reservation will need to pay 30% of the total cost of their stay upfront.

Example 1: Single person in a double room for 3 nights (Full Board)

  • Nightly rate: €75 (single occupancy in double room)
  • Total cost of stay: €75/night * 3 nights = €225
  • Prepayment: €225 * 30% = €67.50

Example 2: Two people in a double room for 5 nights (Full Board)

  • Nightly rate: €50/person * 2 people = €100/night
  • Total cost of stay: €100/night * 5 nights = €500
  • Prepayment: €500 * 30% = €150

Remember: This is just a prepayment. You need to pay the remaining balance upon arrival.
